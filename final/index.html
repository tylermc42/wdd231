Welcome to the Acent and Aclaim Neighborhoods in the Jorde Farms Community

Nestled in the growing town of Queen Creek, AZ, at the intersection of Ellsworth and Germann, this up-and-coming neighborhood is quickly taking shape as a vibrant and welcoming community. With over 300 homes already built and more on the way, the area offers a perfect blend of modern living and outdoor amenities. Residents enjoy access to a sparkling community pool, a scenic park, and lush grass-breeze areas that provide a refreshing escape. As development continues, the neighborhood is set to become one of Queen Creek’s most desirable places to call home, offering both tranquility and convenience in a prime location.
